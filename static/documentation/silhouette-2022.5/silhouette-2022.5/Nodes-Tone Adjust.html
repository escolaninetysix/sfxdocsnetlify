silhouette-2022.5 : Nodes : Tone Adjust
 
Tone Adjust
Description
Tone Adjust approximates the appearance of high dynamic range images by adjusting the tonal values. Specifically, detail is recovered from the darker portions of the images and can optionally be denoised.
Node Group
Color.
Controls
Amount
Lightens shadows to reveal more detail. Take care not to over apply this setting and reveal image noise.
DeNoise
Removes film grain and noise in the shadow areas.
Position
Selects the shadow values to be adjusted.
Range
Controls the range of values to be used for the shadows. A higher Range value considers more values as shadows.
