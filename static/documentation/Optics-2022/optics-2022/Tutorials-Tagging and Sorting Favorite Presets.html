Tagging and Sorting Favorite Presets
Presets can be tagged as a Favorite allowing them to be sorted separately in the Presets window as well as in the Favorites tab of the Filters window.
1 Apply a filter with presets.
2 Tag a preset as a Favorite by selecting the preset and pressing the Toggle Favorite icon located at the top right of the Presets window.
Presets tagged as a favorite display a yellow star at the top right of the preset.
3 To sort the Presets window by Favorites, select Favorites in the Presets pop-up menu.
