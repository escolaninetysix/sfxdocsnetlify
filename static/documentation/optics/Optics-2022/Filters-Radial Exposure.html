Optics-2022 : Filters : Radial Exposure
 
Radial Exposure
Description
Lightens and/or darkens the center or edges of an image to correct lens vignetting.
Category
Lens.
Controls
Exposure
Edges
Lightens or darkens the edges of the image.
Center
Lightens or darkens the center of the image.
Spot
A radial gradient is used to lighten or darken the edges or center of the image. Go to the Spot section of Common Filter Controls to see how the Spot controls work.
