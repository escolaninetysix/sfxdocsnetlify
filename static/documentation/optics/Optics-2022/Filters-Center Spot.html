Optics-2022 : Filters : Center Spot
 
Center Spot
Description
Center Spot
Diffuses and blurs distracting backgrounds while keeping a center spot in focus. The center spot can be moved, sized and the amount of blur can be controlled.
Warm Center Spot
Combines the benefits of Center Spot with a warming filter making it ideal for portraits and skintones.
Category
Diffusion/Blurs.
Controls
Presets
To select a preset, pick one from the Presets window. If you would like to view presets from a different category, use the pop-up menu at the top left of the Presets window.
Blur
Sets how much the image is blurred.
Spot
A spot in the form of a radial gradient is used to control where blur is added to the image. Go to the Spot section of Common Filter Controls to see how the Spot controls work.
Temperature
Applies a warming filter to the image. Go to the Temperature section of Common Filter Controls to see how the Temperature controls work.
