Optics-2022 : Filters : Overexpose
 
Overexpose
Description
Overexpose simulates the overexposure that occurs when a film camera is stopped.
Category
Light.
Controls
Presets
To select a preset, pick one from the Presets window.
Amount
Controls the amount of overexposure.
Intensity
Sets the intensity of the overexposure.
Blur
Sets the softness of the overexposure.
