Introduction to Sapphire Plug-ins for OFX

Sapphire Plug-ins for OFX is a package of image processing and synthesis effects for use with Open FX compatible products such as Nuke, Toxic, Fusion, Baselight, and Film Master. It includes over 250 plug-ins, each with many options and parameters which can be adjusted and animated for an unlimited range of results. Sapphire LensFlare Plug-ins for OFX is a package of two LensFlare Plug-ins for OFX for use with Open FX compatible products such as Nuke, Toxic, Fusion, Baselight, and Film Master.
