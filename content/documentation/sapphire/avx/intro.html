Introduction to Sapphire Plug-ins v2023.0 for Avid Products

Sapphire Plug-ins is a package of image processing and synthesis effects for use with Avid AVX products including Media Composer, Symphony, Xpress Pro, Newscutter, and DS.

It includes over 250 AVX 2 plug-ins, each with many parameters which can be adjusted and animated for an unlimited range of results.
