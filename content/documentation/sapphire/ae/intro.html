Introduction to Sapphire Plug-ins for Adobe After Effects

Sapphire Plug-ins is a package of image processing and synthesis effects for use with Adobe After Effects and compatible products. It includes over 250 plug-ins, each with many options and parameters which can be adjusted and animated for an unlimited range of results. Sapphire LensFlare Plug-ins for Adobe After Effects is a package of two LensFlare Plug-ins for use with Adobe After Effects and compatible products such as Premiere Pro.
