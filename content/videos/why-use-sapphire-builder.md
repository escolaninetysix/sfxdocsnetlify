Sapphire Builder allows you to create custom effects and transitions from existing Sapphire effects and presets. It’s a node-based workflow that has changed how a lot of editors and artist work, producing endless possibilities for combining effects and simplifying workflow. In this tutorial by John Dickinson of Motionworks.net, learn how to use the Sapphire Builder to create custom looks and speed up your workflow.
