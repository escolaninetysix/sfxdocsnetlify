In this in-depth tutorial, VFX artist Lars Wemmje covers **Mocha Pro**'s PowerMesh planar tracking workflow for NUKE users. For warped and organic surface tracking, PowerMesh is a fast and easy-to-use alternative to Smart Vectors and optical flow-based workflows.

Mocha Pro 2021 can be run as an OFX plugin inside Nuke. Track sub-planar surface motion in Mocha Pro, then export to Nuke via the Alembic format.
