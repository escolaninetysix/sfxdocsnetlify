{ "date": "2014-08-06T12:47:00-04:00", "tags": [ "Mocha Pro", "Support FAQ" ], "title": "How do I share my custom keyboard shortcuts?" } Our custom user keyboard shortcuts are designed to be shared, but if you want to share the default settings (AE, Nuke, Etc) you'll need to make a copy of them first. You can find your saved keyboard profiles here: Linux: ~/.config/Imagineer Systems Ltd/ Mac OS: ~/Library/Application Support/Imagineer Systems Ltd/ Windows: [YourUserName]\AppData\Roaming\Imagineer Systems Ltd
