{ "date": "2014-07-01T18:36:00-04:00", "tags": [ "Mocha Pro", "Support FAQ" ], "title": "How do I open Mocha AE that ships with Adobe After Effects CS6 or Creative Cloud?" } Mocha AE ships free within Adobe After Effects CS4 through CreativeCloud. To launch, within After Effects: select the layer you want to track, go to Animation>Track in Mocha AE.
