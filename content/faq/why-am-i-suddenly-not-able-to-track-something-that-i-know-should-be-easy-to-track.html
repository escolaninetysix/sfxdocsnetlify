{ "date": "2014-07-01T18:41:00-04:00", "tags": [ "Mocha Pro", "Support FAQ" ], "title": "Why can I not track something that I know should be easy to track?" } Understanding the planar tracker is key to getting a good track. Increasing the Min % of Pixel Used in the track tab can also help. If your tracker is simply not working properly, there could bea corrupt file in your cache. Delete the contents of your cache folder. You can find your cache folder in your Preferences: File>Preferences wherever you have set it to, but usually somewhere like “C:/MoTemp/”. Delete the cache folder contents and restart Mocha.
