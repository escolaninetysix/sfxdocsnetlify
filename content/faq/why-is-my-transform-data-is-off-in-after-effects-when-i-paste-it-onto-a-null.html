{ "date": "2014-07-01T18:42:00-04:00", "tags": [ "Mocha Pro", "Support FAQ" ], "title": "Why is my transform data off in After Effects when I paste it onto a null?" } If you are using Mocha3.2.1 and below only it is because the anchor point is also driving the null. Turn off the anchor point and your track and null should line up perfectly. InV4 we removedthe anchor keys for our AE Transform Data export.
